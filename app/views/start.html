Who can use this

Traders who need to declare imported goods. You cannot declare your imports online if you use Community Service Providers (CSP) inventory linked software systems.

You will need:

  • your Government Gateway details
  • your EORI number
  • the commodity codes of your goods
  • the procedure code, which is the reason for the import
  • details of your goods and where they came from
